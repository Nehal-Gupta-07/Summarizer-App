Summarize any article easily

SumUp is designed to help you quickly and efficiently summarize lengthy text documents, articles, or any other piece of written content.

Features

Web Scraping

This feature automatically extracts the title , keywords and publish date, as well as summarizes the text from news articles and blog posts so you have everything in one place.

Text Summarizing

This feature takes any long text as input and returns the key sentences from it, helping you summarize information into a concise, easy to digest piece of text.

Word Cloud Generator

This feature takes any long text as input and generates a word cloud from it, with the most frequently used and important words being more prominent!

Named Entity Recognizer

This feature takes any long text as input and returns all the names , organisations , products and geopolitical entites , giving you the keywords from the passage!

About

In the sheer amount of information that bombards Internet users from all sides, hardly anyone wants to devote their valuable time to reading long texts. SumUp analyzes any piece of text and summarizes it automatically, in a way that makes it easy for you to read, understand and act on. SumUp eliminates ads, popups, graphics, and other online distractions to give you a clean and focused reading experience. SumUp selects the most relevant points from a text (while filtering out weak arguments, baseless speculation, flashy phrases, attention wasters etc) so you can get the gist of what is said quickly, without having to go through all the paragraphs.

Simply select the feature you want to use. Then, enter the URL or text that you want summarized and let SumUp do the rest!

You could also check out our other features, Word Cloud Generator and Named Entity Recognizer as well!
